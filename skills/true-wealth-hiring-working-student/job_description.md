# Working Student — Asset Management (20%)

**True Wealth** is the Swiss pioneer in online wealth management, experiencing rapid growth.
With over 40,000 clients entrusting us with their savings and investments, we are committed
to making investing simpler, more transparent, and more accessible. Our mission is to deliver
exceptional value by combining digitization with cost-efficiency, empowering our clients to
take control of their financial future.

Investing can be complex, even intimidating, for many. We want to change that — and that's
where you come in.

We are looking for a **Working Student (20%, one full day per week)** to strengthen our
Asset Management (IM) team. If you are interested in applied investments at a fintech
company, you are encouraged to apply!

---

## Your Responsibilities

**Trading & Execution support (30%)**
- Support portfolio rebalancing activities, ensuring efficiency, accuracy, and adherence to
  internal protocols.

**IM project participation (70%)**
- Systematize academic research on financial and business topics of direct interest to us.
- Conduct fund screening and analysis.
- Collect and process financial data.

---

## Your Profile

**Required**
- At least two completed semesters towards a degree in Economics or Finance.
- Proven knowledge of investment management and on-order-book trading (completed coursework
  or professional/hobby experience counts).
- Basic data manipulation skills: spreadsheets, pivot tables, visualization.
- Strong interest in financial markets.

**Nice to have**
- Prior experience with the Bloomberg terminal or similar market data systems.
- Basic familiarity with modern IT: command line, version control, SQL queries.

---

## Why True Wealth?

At True Wealth, we offer a dynamic and supportive environment where your work will directly
contribute to reshaping how people in Switzerland approach investing. Our core values are
transparency, simplicity, and customer-centric innovation.
