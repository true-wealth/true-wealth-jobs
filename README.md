## How applying via Claude works

**Prerequisites:** `git`, [Claude Code](https://claude.ai/code) installed and set up.

1. Download or `clone` this repository
2. Open Claude Code in the downloaded directory
3. Prompt it with "I want to apply to [POSITION AT ] True Wealth"

> For example:
> ```bash
> git clone https://github.com/true-wealth/true-wealth-jobs
> cd true-wealth-jobs
> claude "I want to apply for the Working Student – Asset Management role at True Wealth"
> ```

4. Claude walks you through a short application form collecting the necessary information...
5. ...and runs a short bash script to submit your application directly to True Wealth over HTTPS.

Prefer email? Send your CV to **jobs@truewealth.ch** — you will not be disadvantaged
for choosing that route.

## Privacy & Data Handling

This section explains what happens with your data, what is and isn't in our control, and how to apply through a conventional channel if you prefer.

### During the conversation (before you submit)

You decide what to share. The conversation is between you and Claude (the AI), running on your machine with your Anthropic account. You decide which questions to answer and how much detail to provide.

Your data goes to Anthropic, not to True Wealth. When you talk to Claude, your messages are sent to Anthropic's servers under the terms of your own Anthropic subscription. True Wealth has no contractual relationship with Anthropic for this conversation, no access to it, and no control over how Anthropic processes it. This part of the data processing is outside True Wealth's sphere of influence.

Since you are using your own Anthropic account, Anthropic's privacy policy and terms of service apply to the conversation: https://www.anthropic.com/legal/privacy.

Be deliberate about sensitive information. You do not need to share — and we ask Claude not to ask for — health information, religious or political views, trade union membership, biometric data, criminal record information, or other particularly sensitive personal data ("besonders schützenswerte Personendaten" under Swiss data protection law). If such information is not relevant to your application, please don't include it. If you do choose to include it, please be aware that it will be processed by Anthropic during the conversation and, if you submit it, by True Wealth thereafter.

Don't include third-party data without consent. Avoid mentioning identifiable colleagues, references, or other third parties in detail unless you have their permission.

### When you submit the summary

Once you submit, True Wealth becomes the data controller for the data you send (the summary, and optionally the full conversation transcript if you choose to include it).

The submitted data is stored in *Attio CRM* (hosted in the UK under a Data Processing Agreement with Standard Contractual Clauses in place) and in *BambooHR*, our applicant tracking system. It may be combined with information from our standard recruiting process (e.g. an LLM-assisted CV ranking via BambooHR — see our privacy policy for details).

The Claude-generated summary is one element among many. No candidate is rejected based solely on the Claude conversation summary. Recruiting decisions are made by our recruiting team, with full review of all available information.

### Retention

Your application data is retained for 3 months after the position has been filled, unless you have agreed to longer retention for future opportunities. After that, it is deleted.

### Your rights
You have the right to access, correct, and delete your personal data, and to object to its processing. To exercise these rights, contact us at jobs@truewealth.ch.
For full details on how True Wealth processes applicant data — including recipients, international data transfers, and your rights under the Swiss Federal Act on Data Protection (FADP / DSG) — please see our privacy policy: https://www.truewealth.ch/en/data-protection-declaration.
